Ceremonial Koi

Our most prized koi for ceremonies and festivals.

"HANTU"

25' Ginrin Matsukawabake

Hantu, named for its coloration and isolated nature, is a behemoth-classed koi. Ideal for ceremonial blessings, especailly in regards to the Lkywean god Voun.

"HAVIV"

5' Soragoi

Haviv, a moderate-sized koi, contrasts well with the dark waters of the Endormi River. A longstanding choice for the Festival of the Seafarer, Haviv is good with children and enjoys being fed.

"IEME"

17.5'' Hi Utsuri Butterfly

Ieme, a 450-year old male Hi Utsuri, has spanned 9 generations of koi breeders. He is considered to be good luck and welcomed at the All Souls Day celebration in Selenlaga.
