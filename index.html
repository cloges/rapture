Highest quality Koi from Rapture, Senostra

Located on northern Haya Land Bank in the Husnya district of Rapture, Senostra, our generational koi fish are loved by commoner and king alike. Raised with generations of experience and Yastuo Rasinar guidance, our koi have been used for the New Sunset Festivals and Council-led foreign cultural exchange projects.
