Rapture Koi farm is Senostra's oldest koi farm, going back to the introduction of the Husnya to the Governing Council. King Star Mahawai granted our founder, Jayatung, land along the North Haya River with the task of creating a strong cultural contribution from the Husnya to the overall council alliance. We continue to fulfill this mission with our eleventh-generation farm, bringing the utmost care and dedication to our work. Our koi have been featured at major festivals throughout the nation, and are a staple at the Days of Migration, where our behemoth koi travel to sacred spawning grounds in Tboureng providence; they have been given to foreign kings and emperors as gifts. Our koi can be found in Oxazural, Mithrissia, and The Unending Principles, as well as several provinces throughout the world.
